I came home with a full heart, where she was waiting for me — my little wellspring of energy and creativity. Falling asleep in her arms as she told me I'm "the best mum" and that she wished she'd been there to see your joy — that was the most beautiful reward I could have received. Together we looked through every photo, every video, and every Identity Canvas created that evening. Her verdict? The colourful feathers were, indeed, the stars of the night! 🪶💜

## What is Identity Canvas?

For Women's Day, we wanted to create something different — an event just for us, as women. Two hours where we set aside the "I have to" and sat together at a table of rediscovery. With The Gifted Piggy, each participant created an **Identity Canvas** — a visual map of the soul on canvas. We painted, decorated, and glued answers to questions that made us smile or reflect:

- *Who am I beyond my role as a mother?*
- *What truly energises me and what truly relaxes me?*
- *What did I dream of becoming when I was little, and what are my dreams now?*

## A magical evening

It was a magical evening — one that began with tears of emotion and ended with an immense sense of fulfilment. We had **Roxana Postolache** by our side, a psychotherapist and parenting specialist, who warmly guided us through the questions that weigh on all of us at the end of the day: what do we do with children who simply won't do their homework, how do we cope on those days when we feel we just can't go on, how do we find calm when everything around us feels like chaos.

It was a warm, heartfelt conversation — soul to soul — that helped us rediscover the balance and inner strength we so desperately need.
